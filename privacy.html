Every Wall Falls

Privacy Policy

The short version: we don’t collect anything.

Last updated: 18 July 2026

In plain terms: Every Wall Falls does not collect, store on our servers, transmit, share, or sell any personal information. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no tracking. The game runs entirely on your device.

Every Wall Falls (“the game,” “the app”) is made by Bagel Arcade (“we,” “us”). This policy explains how the app handles information — which, deliberately, is as little as technically possible.

Information we collect

None. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information. We don’t require an account, and we don’t use advertising, analytics, cookies, tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or third‑party data‑collection SDKs of any kind.

Data stored on your device

To let you pick up where you left off, the game saves data locally on your device — your campaign progress, in‑game settings (such as sound), and achievements. This information:

  • stays on your device and is never sent to us or anyone else;
  • is not tied to your identity;
  • can be erased at any time by resetting your progress in‑game, clearing the app’s storage, or deleting the app.

Network use

The game is designed to run offline. It does not connect to any servers we operate, because we don’t operate any. If you use the optional “share your result” feature, the game copies a short text summary to your device’s clipboard when you tap it — nothing is transmitted to us, and you choose where to paste it.

App stores and your device platform

You download the app through Apple’s App Store or Google Play. Those platforms — and your device’s operating system — may collect standard technical information such as download counts, purchase records, or crash reports under their own privacy policies. That processing is outside our control and is governed by Apple and Google. We do not receive any personal information from them.

Children’s privacy

Because the app collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children. It is suitable for players of all ages and complies with children’s privacy laws such as COPPA and the GDPR by simply not gathering data.

Changes to this policy

If the app ever changes how it handles information, we will update this page and revise the “Last updated” date above before the change takes effect.
